Lingayen Products


Furniture


Local furniture manufacturers in Lingayen are currently enjoying robust growth given strong export and local demand.

One of the famouse manufacturers is the D'Original Bartolome Furniture SINCE1932 and was the first furniture shop in Lingayen, Pangasinan started in 1932 founded by Teodoro Bartolome.

It was continued by his daughter Concordia Bartolome-Lopez and her husband named Alonzo Lopez in 1976 after the death of Teodoro Bartolome.

Now It is owned and management by Teddy Bartolome Lopez the eldest son of Concordia Bartolome-Lopez and Alonzo Lopez. Alonzo Lopez has made innovations by revolutionizing the concepts of customizing and restoring furniture's most of them new and old costumers who bought from them.

Their store is located at Tonton Road, Tonton, Lingayen, 2401 Pangasinan.

Coconut Midribs


The walis tingting is a stickbroom made with coconut frond spines, and great for cleaning outdoor areas, where fallen leaves can clutter up an otherwise immaculate street.

In fact, they can be found practically everywhere. Before commercial cleaning tools made of plastic and cloth came into the market,the walis was what kept the floors of Filipino homes clean. Even today, these tools are still used in homes and establishments throughout the Philippines.
